Reaction Keeper

Reaction Keeper is a program which lets you quickly save links to popular images. You can name the link and also attach tags to it.
This app is perfect for those times when you're browsing Reddit/Facebook/forums and you've got a good meme image in mind. Instead of hitting up Google and looking for it, you can just fire up Reaction Keeper, enter some tags and you're good to go.
